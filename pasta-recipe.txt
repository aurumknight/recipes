Ingredients:
 - 1 cup of dry pasta
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 
 Directions:
  1. Fill small pot with water and place on stove. Turn the
	 stove heat onto max to let the water boil.
  
  2. When the water is boiling, turn the heat to medium and 
     pour your pasta into the pot.
	 
  3. Sprinkle the salt into the pot with the pasta.
  
  4. Stir the pasta every minute for 7 minutes or until the 
     noodles are fully cooked.
	 
  5. Strain the water from the pot and put the noodles into
     a bowl.
